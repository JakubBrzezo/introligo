# Product Service

The Product Service manages the entire product catalog including inventory tracking, pricing, and hierarchical categories. It supports complex product attributes through flexible map fields.

## Key Capabilities

- **Product Catalog** - SKU-based product management with rich metadata
- **Inventory Tracking** - Real-time stock levels with low-stock alerts
- **Hierarchical Categories** - Nested category structure for organization
- **Dynamic Pricing** - Regular and promotional pricing with time-based discounts
- **Advanced Search** - Filter by category, price range, tags, and full-text search
- **Warehouse Integration** - Location tracking for distributed inventory
